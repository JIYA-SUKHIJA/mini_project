Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new task?
Go to the main dashboard and click the "Add Task" button at the top right of your board layout context.

Q: Is my task state stored securely?
Yes, all workflow objects are sandboxed locally into your explicit account storage index maps.

Data Protection & Integrity Manifest

Your workspace data integrity is our highest priority. We use local state isolation protocols to store data without structural transit leaks.

  • No cloud indexing maps are created without manual sync.
  • Account parameter details are client-encrypted locally.
  • Wiping data cleans your encryption profile cache completely.
